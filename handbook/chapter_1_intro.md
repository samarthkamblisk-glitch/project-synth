# Chapter 1: The Agent Singularity

## The Shift
The workforce is changing. It is no longer just humans. It is **Hybrid**.
*   **The Old Way:** Humans doing work, supported by tools.
*   **The New Way:** Agents doing work, supervised by humans.

This shift creates a **Governance Vacuum**.
Your Engineering team builds the agents. Your Ops team runs them. But who **governs** them?

## The Risk Vector
An unguarded agent is a loaded gun pointed at your P&L.
*   **The $1 Refund:** A support bot refunds a customer $1. Fine.
*   **The Loop:** A support bot refunds 1,000 customers $1 in 5 minutes because of a prompt injection. **Not Fine.**
