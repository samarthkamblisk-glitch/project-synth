About SynthForce

We’re a team of Cornell entrepreneurs building the HR layer for AI agents.

Mission: To give every business leader the same visibility and control over their AI agents as they have over their human employees.

Vision: A world where AI agents are managed with the same rigor, fairness, and accountability as human teams.

Why we’re doing this: AI agents are becoming synthetic employees—but they’re managed like infrastructure. That leads to financial bleed, silent liability, and zombie agents. We’re fixing that.

The Founder

SynthForce was founded by a Cornell student who saw the management gap firsthand while working with AI‑powered startups. The project is backed by Cornell’s entrepreneurship community (Blackstone Launchpad, eLab, Rev).
